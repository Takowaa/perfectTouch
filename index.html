Eyebrow

Ombré brows gives a similar effect to applying powder or pencil on your brows. However, this technique can be done as light as natural, or as bold and beautiful, it depends on your personal preference. The procedure is performed using a micro-pigmentation machine and gradually builds defined shape and colour for the brows. The result for these techniques tend to last longer , less painful than microblading and is suitable for all individuals, especially those with oily skin type.

Tattoo removal

Non Laser tattoo removal is an option to unwanted or badly done old tattoo. Unlike laser the removal solution workers gently and delicately. The method helps to deliver a lactic acid based fluid underneath to the layer in which the ink is trapped. It is required multiple sessions.

Eyeliner

Permanent eyeliner is a form of micro pigmentation. It is a practice of applying colour on the upper eye liner to highlight the eyes and create definition. If you live an active and busy lifestyle, permanent make up allows you to get up and go without the hassle of applying regular makeup. Cosmetic tattooing is also ideal for people with bad vision and unable to apply eyeliner themselves. You can choose between a natural lash enhancement to give the illusion of a fuller lash line or you can dare to be more dramatic with a thicker design.

Lip

Lip tattoo for those who are after a natural lip or want to emulate the colour of your favourite lipstick, permanent lip colour can breathe new life into your face. It is defines lip lines, corrects symmetry, and creates the illusion of fuller lips. It can also de-emphasise age lines and correct fading colour as a result of ageing.
